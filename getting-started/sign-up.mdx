Getting started with Neptureon is quick and easy.

## Create Your Account

1. Visit [neptureon.com](https://neptureon.com)
2. Click **Log In / Sign Up** at the top right
3. Click **Launch Neptureon** in the middle

You'll see several login options:
- **Google** - Sign in with your Google account
- **Discord** - Sign in with your Discord account
- **Email** - Sign up with your email address

Choose your preferred method and you'll be ready to start tracking accounts across all platforms.
